Traffic Safety Solutions

Our Traffic and Road Safety Solutions meet international standards and best practice both in design and installation ensuring practical usage, durability and project sustainability. Our goal is to be a major contributor in the move towards zero deaths on our roads, highways and parking areas.

Road Surface Markings

With specialist equipment and well-trained personnel, we execute durable projects for the Federal and State Governments, local authorities, commercial clients and private customers with marking systems available to suit a wide variety of surface types.

We carry out comprehensive highway projects, which include but are not restricted to edge lines, center lines, lane lines, stop and give-way markings, nus and cycle lanes, chevrons, waiting and loading restrictions, parking bays, hatched box junctions and other floor symbols.

 

Car Park Solutions

Car park solutions are an essential part of an effective parking lot system. At Rocsol, we have gained experience in planning, designing, installation and marking of various indoor and outdoor car parks for schools, hotels, airports, sports and recreation centres, industrial and public buildings. Standard parking solutions include car park markings, vehicle stoppers, speed bumps, corner guards, convex mirrors and signage.

Our design team is available to ensure that services are tailor-made to meet unique expectations. Cold paint, thermoplastic material and pre-formed thermoplastic are our major materials for markings.

Car park markings commonly include delineations, disabled parking, regulatory signs, customized lettering, arrows and directional signs.

Traffic Light Control Systems

We supply and install a large variety of smart traffic management devices that are phase-expandable, near maintenance-free, durable (rust resistant), eco-friendly and low energy consuming.

They can be powered by traditional electric or solar depending on the prevailing environmental and project requirements.

A standard signalized intersection light consists of LED signal heads, galvanized steel poles, countdown timers pedestrian lights/signals, programmable controllers with GPS, vehicle presence and density detectors, solar panels with inverters and batteries (for solar powered variants).

Road Traffic Signs

Road traffic signs are installed at roadsides and overhead, as a means of passing useful information to road users. At Rocsol, we offer a full selection of high quality road traffic signs ranging from warnings, regulatory and informational signs.

All signs are produced with aluminum, reinforced with metal braces and covered in reflective sheets to endure retro-reflectivity at night. These sheets are also available in diamond with higher sensitivity and their use is dependent on the type, location and purpose of the sign. They are available in various shapes, sizes and colours and are weather/rust resistant with a lifespan of at least 10-15 years.

Street Identification and House Number Signs

Street identification (naming) and house numbering are an integral part of organized societies and are used to identify roads that have been registered and approved; Rocsol offers a wide variety of designs and specifications for this purpose.

We use the best quality of materials (aluminum reinforced in metal braces and covered with diamond grade reflective sheets) in order to provide information more efficiently to road users and the general public.

We produce and install signs that have a guaranteed lifespan of at least 5-10 years as they are weather and rust proof.

Traffic Calming Devices

Traffic calming involves the installation of safety solutions to slow or reduce traffic speed in order to enhance road safety both for pedestrians and motorists. At Rocsol, we deliver top of the range traffic calming devices to suit the peculiar needs of our varied clients.

These devices are high quality, cost effective, customizable, durable and environmentally friendly.
